History lesson

Do you want to learn more about this ancient board game? It goes back 1500 years in the heart of Asia. The earliest known predecessor, called "Chaturanga" originated in India. Chess was taken up by the Muslim world and subsequently spread to southern Europe. The game evolved roughly into its current form by about 1500 AD.
